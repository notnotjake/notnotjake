Dear Mom,

Scroll

Hundreds of stars across the night sky

Hundreds of shells on the ocean shores

Hundreds of birds that go singing by

Hundreds of paths through the forest floors

Hundreds of dewdrops to greet the dawn

Hundreds of bees in the purple clover

Hundreds butterflies on the lawn

But only one mother the whole world over.

Thank You.

With Love,

Mothers Day. May 14th, 2023.

Poem modified from "Only One Mother" by George Cooper
